About this website

Vitamin A supplementation (VAS) is a widely recognized high-impact and cost-effective intervention that can reduce child mortality by 24%. In countries where HKI works, this means a potential of 269,991 lives saved in 2014 alone. This website shows coverage of vitamin A supplementation measured by post event coverage surveys and tally sheet data to track progress against our goal to reach 90% of children 6-59 months with vitamin A supplementation.
